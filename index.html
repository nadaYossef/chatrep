Telecom SR Assistant

Welcome

This portal helps extract business rules and detect or predict SR (service request) anomalies for a telecom company. Use the navigation to manage rules, read documentation, and view SR explosion warnings. A chatbot will be added later.

Color theme: rosy / reddish for clear visual priority and alerts.

Business Rules

Business rules define how to inspect SR tickets in a queue and classify actions: escalate, reject, handle, or transfer. Rules are editable by team members here. Rules are stored locally in your browser; for multi-user collaboration host this site and connect to a shared backend database.
